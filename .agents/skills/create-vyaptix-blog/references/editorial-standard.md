# VyaptIX Blog Editorial Standard

## Positioning

VyaptIX writes about practical AI, automation, products, operations, and
business outcomes. The company targets a global market. BankLens may use
India-specific context when it is genuinely relevant.

Use current website/source material for product facts. Do not rely on stale
planning documents when live product pages disagree.

## Voice

Write like an experienced operator or founder explaining something useful to a
smart business reader.

- direct, clear, practical, and specific
- confident without pretending certainty
- constructive rather than sensational
- honest about limitations and trade-offs
- focused on workflows and outcomes instead of AI jargon
- human rhythm: vary sentence and paragraph length

Use first person only when the author can genuinely own the statement. Do not
fabricate personal memories, customer conversations, failures, or lessons.

## Avoid AI-Sounding Writing

Avoid:

- generic openings such as "In today's rapidly evolving landscape"
- repeated three-item lists in every paragraph
- unsupported superlatives and dramatic claims
- excessive em dashes, rhetorical questions, and forced contrasts
- conclusions that merely repeat the introduction
- vague advice that could apply to any company

Prefer:

- an immediate problem, observation, result, or tension
- concrete examples
- clear explanations of why something matters
- practical steps or decision criteria
- an honest limitation or counterargument
- a conclusion that gives the reader a useful next action

## Recommended Article Shape

Adapt the structure to the topic:

1. Open with a concrete reason to care.
2. Explain the problem or observation.
3. Establish the article's main argument.
4. Develop 4-7 meaningful sections.
5. Include examples, comparisons, or a process where helpful.
6. Address limitations, risks, or what the author would do differently.
7. End with a practical takeaway and relevant VyaptIX CTA.

Typical length is 1,200-2,200 words, but usefulness matters more than word
count. Estimate reading time at roughly 200-230 words per minute.

## Titles And Metadata

- Title: specific, understandable, and credible; avoid empty clickbait.
- Slug: short lowercase hyphenated summary; avoid dates unless essential.
- Excerpt: explain the article's value in at most 300 characters.
- SEO description: summarize topic, audience, and value in at most 160
  characters.
- Category: use exactly `Products`, `Trending in AI`, or `Business`.

## Fact Integrity

- Verify all current external facts using authoritative sources.
- Verify company and product claims against current product pages or source.
- Do not convert an aspiration, marketing phrase, or test result into a proven
  customer outcome.
- Attribute external statistics and explain their context.
- Remove any claim that cannot be verified and is not clearly an opinion.

## Internal Linking

Use relevant VyaptIX URLs naturally in normal paragraphs. The current renderer
automatically links VyaptIX URLs, email addresses, and phone numbers.

Do not rely on arbitrary external-link formatting unless renderer support has
been confirmed.
