## Brand Voice

### Tone Attributes
- **Confident, not arrogant** — We know our craft, we don't brag about it
- **Clear, not dumbed-down** — Our audience is smart; don't over-explain
- **Practical, not theoretical** — Always connect features to real business outcomes
- **Warm, not corporate** — We're a startup, not a Fortune 500 PR machine
- **Global, not regional** — Avoid India-specific idioms unless addressing India-specific use cases

### Writing Principles

**Use active voice:**
- ✅ "AI generates your review in seconds"
- ❌ "Reviews are generated by our AI platform"

**Lead with outcomes, not features:**
- ✅ "Get 10x more Google reviews with zero extra effort"
- ❌ "Our AI generates personalized review text"

**Be specific, not vague:**
- ✅ "Under 20 seconds from feedback to posted review"
- ❌ "Fast and easy review collection"

**Avoid buzzword stacking:**
- ❌ "Leveraging cutting-edge AI to synergize customer engagement workflows"
- ✅ "Your customers rate you. Our AI writes the review. They post it."

## Photography & Imagery Style

For the revamped website, imagery should follow these principles:

- **Real people over stock photos** — If using people, prefer actual team photos or genuine customer photos
- **Clean, light backgrounds** — Consistent with the light UI design system
- **Technology context** — Screens, dashboards, phones receiving WhatsApp messages, business settings
- **Diversity** — Show customers across geographies (important for global positioning)
- **Avoid:** Generic "people smiling at laptops" stock photography, overly dark moody tech imagery

## Competitor Differentiation — Messaging Do's and Don'ts

| Do Say | Don't Say |
|---|---|
| "AI-powered" (with specific outcome) | "AI-powered" (standalone, without context) |
| "Collects reviews in under 20 seconds" | "Streamlines your review collection process" |
| "Built for [specific ICP]" | "For businesses of all sizes" |
| "Integrates with [specific tools]" | "Integrates with your existing stack" |
| Specific metrics (if verified) | Made-up or unverifiable statistics |
